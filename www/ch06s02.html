2. JBoss - JBoss Enterprise Middleware

参考Java安装

  1. 下载安装 JBoss

    cd /usr/local/src/				
wget http://nchc.dl.sourceforge.net/sourceforge/jboss/jboss-5.0.0.Beta2.zip
unzip jboss-5.0.0.Beta2.zip
mv jboss-5.0.0.Beta2 ..
cd ..
ln -s jboss-5.0.0.Beta2 jboss

  2. 运行 Jboss

    cd jboss/bin
chmod +x *.sh
./run.sh
