Q: 运行amavis后,自行退出

A: 首选进入调式模式

[root@linuxas3 amavis]# /usr/local/sbin/amavisd debug

Sep 23 20:58:41 mail.gomi.com.hk amavisd[27570]: TROUBLE in pre_loop_hook: QUARANTINEDIR directory not writable: /var/virusmails at /usr/local/sbin/amavisd line 4982.
Suicide () TROUBLE in pre_loop_hook: QUARANTINEDIR directory not writable: /var/virusmails at /usr/local/sbin/amavisd line 4982.			
解决方法
# chown amavis.amavis -R /var/virusmails
