blacklist/whitelist

Example 5. 测试黑名单

测试方法：将其它ISP邮箱加入"blacklist_sender",第一个邮件地址后加回车．

[root@linuxas3 amavis]# echo netkillers@sohu.com >> blacklist_sender
[root@linuxas3 amavis]# cat blacklist_sender
netkillers@sohu.com
[root@linuxas3 amavis]#

然后使用netkillers@sohu.com这个邮箱给自己发封邮件．

注：$final_spam_destiny = D_BOUNCE; # (defaults to D_REJECT)

你会收到一封弹回邮件．发送者是：amavisd-new<postmaster@yourdomain.org>

主题为：Considered UNSOLICITED BULK EMAIL from you
