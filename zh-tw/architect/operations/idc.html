1.4. 接入線路測試

用於評估機房線路.

ping.lst

202.96.134.133
202.96.128.68
...
...

測試腳本

			
#!/bin/bash
for addr in `cat ping.lst`
do
        ping -c 5 -s 128 -W 5 $addr
        echo
        echo
done > ping_report.txt &

將不同機房的出ping_report.txt檔案彙總，然後用是diff對比。例如 vimdiff report1.txt report2.txt.

			
#!/bin/bash
for addr in `cat ping.lst`
do
        traceroute $addr
        echo
        echo
done > traceroute_report.txt &
